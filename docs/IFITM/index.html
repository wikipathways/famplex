IFITM

Interferon inducible transmembrane proteins have diverse roles, including the control of cell proliferation, promotion of homotypic cell adhesion, protection against viral infection, promotion of bone matrix maturation and mineralisation, and mediating germ cell development. (hgnc.genefamily:1598)
